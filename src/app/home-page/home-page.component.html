About Pulse Connect

Health information to you in a heartbeat - bridging the digital divide in rural South African communities

Our Mission

To provide free, culturally relevant health information to rural South African communities through an optimized mobile app and web platform, overcoming barriers of connectivity, literacy, and language.

Key Features

  • ✓ Structured health courses (HIV/AIDS, mental health, first aid) with certification
  • ✓ Multilingual content in isiZulu, isiXhosa, Sesotho and English
  • ✓ Offline accessibility for areas with limited connectivity
  • ✓ AI-powered health chatbot (Botpress) for 24/7 assistance
  • ✓ Community forums for peer-to-peer knowledge sharing
